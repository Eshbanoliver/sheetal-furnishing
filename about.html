The Beginning of Sheetal Furnishing

Founded in 2007, Sheetal Furnishing emerged from a passion for exquisite textile design and a commitment to transforming Indian living spaces.

Our story is one of perseverance and artistic evolution. What started as a boutique showroom in Jaipur has grown into a landmark of luxury furnishing. Over the past 17 years, we have served thousands of families, architects, and designers, building a reputation for uncompromising quality and trust.

We believe that "The Home is Where the Heart is," and our role is to ensure that heart beats in a beautiful, comfortable environment. Our curated collection reflects a blend of traditional Indian heritage and global contemporary trends.
